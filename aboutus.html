About Rexbrew Coffee

Rexbrew Coffee was born from a simple yet powerful love for coffee. In the heart of Rexburg, three lifelong friends decided to transform their passion into a community-driven experience. It all started in a garage with a French press, a dream, and countless late-night brewing experiments.

Our goal wasn’t just to make coffee—we wanted to create a space where people could slow down, connect, and savor life’s moments over a perfectly brewed cup. We spent months sourcing the best beans, testing roasts, and learning the art of espresso. Eventually, Rexbrew Coffee was established as more than just a shop—it became a second home to students, locals, and travelers alike.

Today, Rexbrew is a vibrant coffee community rooted in values of quality, warmth, and authenticity. From pour-overs to pastries, every item is crafted with care. We’re proud to support local farmers and sustainable practices, ensuring that your coffee journey is as ethical as it is enjoyable.

Whether you're a coffee aficionado or just looking for a cozy place to study, we invite you to experience the warmth and hospitality of Rexbrew Coffee. Because to us, every cup is an opportunity to connect.
